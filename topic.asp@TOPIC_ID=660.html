в visio есть понятия annotation и comment
вот что написано про них в хелпе
-----------------------------------------------------
About annotations

You can glue the endpoints of many of the shapes on the Annotation stencil to connection points on drawing shapes so that the annotation stays connected if you move the shape to which it's glued.

If your annotations aren't gluing to connections points, click Snap & Glue on the Tools menu and make sure that Connection Points is checked under Glue to in the Snap & Glue dialog box.

----------------------------------------
Add, edit, or delete a comment

Comments can be inserted anywhere on a page.

To add a comment to the current drawing page, on the Insert menu, click Comment.
Type a comment of any length, and then click outside the comment text box.
To reposition the comment, click the comment tag and drag it to another location.
To view comment text, click the comment tag.
To edit or delete a comment, right-click the comment tag.
Note You cannot format the text of a comment.
